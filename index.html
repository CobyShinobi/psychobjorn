About Me

Psycho Björn is ran by Channae Appel, who resides in Logan, Utah. Mother of 3, and wife to her sweetheart, Johan. Channae is an Esthetician, but wanted to pick up a hobby when the kids went to bed. She had lots of ideas to make funky, sparkly, and neon jewelry, but didn’t know where to begin. She started researching different ways, and what to buy to get started. It became way more than a hobby, it became a new LOVE. Every piece is handmade, and made with care. All items are customizable!
